## KEY UX INSIGHTS ACROSS ALL PERSONAS

### 1. **Mobile-First is Critical**
- Miguel (90% mobile) and Lisa (increasing mobile use) need seamless phone experiences
- Sarah wants desktop dashboards but needs mobile alerts
- James needs desktop for case work but mobile notifications

### 2. **Clarity Over Features**
- All personas want SIMPLE interfaces ("tell me what to do")
- Legal jargon must be translated to plain English
- Fewer clicks = higher completion rates

### 3. **Proactive Communication Beats Reactive**
- Everyone hates chasing for updates
- Automated notifications reduce anxiety and workload
- SMS preferred over email for time-sensitive updates

### 4. **Financial Transparency Builds Trust**
- Miguel needs payment plans and clear pricing
- James needs guaranteed payment
- Lisa needs to see ROI and cost justification
- Sarah needs to justify budget to executives

### 5. **Minimal Onbo
